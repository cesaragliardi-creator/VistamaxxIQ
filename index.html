Market rationale — detailed

Regulatory pressure

IEC 62821 mandates halogen-free cables in EU public buildings by 2027. REACH restrictions eliminate DINP/DEHP plasticisers in flexible PVC. Estimated 850,000 tonnes of PVC cable compound affected annually in Europe alone.

Technical advantage

Vistamaxx 1120 at MFR 2 g/10min delivers sufficient melt strength for cable extrusion. With 60% ATH filler loading, passes UL94 V-0. Density 0.882 g/cm³ vs PVC at 1.35 g/cm³ — 35% weight reduction in jacketed cable assemblies.

Market size & KTA estimate

Global low-voltage cable market: 4.2M tonnes/yr jacket compound. Capturing 0.37% share = 15.4 KTA Vistamaxx. Primary targets: building wire (EU/Asia), automotive HV harness (EV growth), marine & offshore (SOLAS requirements).
